The Visionary

Colonel Suresh Patil

Founder of Green Thumb Foundation & The "Water Man" of Pune

"We must treat nature with the same discipline and dedication as we do in the army. Protecting our water is protecting our nation."

Colonel Suresh Patil

Featured Story

Colonel Suresh Patil's journey from commanding regiments to commanding excavators for desilting is nothing short of legendary. Watch how a retired army officer became the sentinel of Pune's water security.

Key Initiatives

Khadakwasla Restoration

Spearheaded the massive desilting project, removing millions of cubic meters of silt and restoring the dam's lifeline capacity.

Afforestation Drives

Led campaigns planting over 100,000 trees in the catchment area, creating green lungs for the city and preventing erosion.

People's Movement

Transformed a personal mission into a mass movement, mobilizing thousands of citizens, students, and corporates.

Policy & Advocacy

Actively engaging with government bodies to shape sustainable water management policies for future generations.

Career Highlights

  • 1Commissioned into the Maratha Light Infantry.
  • 2Served in critical operations including 1971 War.
  • 3Retired as a Colonel after decades of service.
  • 4Founded Green Thumb in 1993 post-retirement.
  • 5Pioneered the concept of 'Green Warriors'.
